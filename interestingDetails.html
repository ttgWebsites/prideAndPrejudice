Interesting Details

  • Austen first wrote the novel in the late 18th century and titled it First Impressions, however after struggling to get it published, she made lengthy revisions, changed the title, and it was then she found success.
  • Jane Austen sold the copyright to her novel for a very low price, she had not anticipated it selling well. In doing so she lost a significant amount of money, as it sold considerably well, not only early on, but from then on out.
  • In 2005 a major motion picture version of the novel came out, the protagonist was played by the widely famous and popular Keira Knightley.
