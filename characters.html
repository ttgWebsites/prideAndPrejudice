Characters

  • Mrs. Bennet
  • Mother of 5 (all girls)
  • Sets people up
  • Embarrasses her daughters by scaring away potential suitors
  • Acts on natural instinct
  • Mr. Bennet
  • Very quiet
  • Smartass sometimes
  • More classy than his wife, but still makes bad decisions sometimes
  • Jane Bennet
  • Oldest daughter
  • Beautiful
  • Confuses most people by not showing that much emotion
  • Can't see the bad in anyone
  • Mary Bennet
  • Doesn't go to the social events
  • Never leaves the house
  • Always is readying
  • Her mom wishes she would be more social
  • Elizabeth Bennet
  • Protagonist
  • Second daughter
  • Smartass like her dad
  • Pretty plain
  • Loves Mr. Darcy even though he doesn't love her back
  • Kitty Bennet
  • Fourth daughter
  • Hangs out with Lydia a lot and meets the soldiers
  • Lydia Bennet
  • Youngest
  • The favorite of Mrs. Bennet
  • Makes rash decisions just like her mom
  • The rest of the family blocks her out when she runs off with Wickham
  • George Wickham
  • Childhood friend of Darcy
  • Military officer
  • Tries to use Darcy to get woman to like him
  • Poor
  • Mr. Collins
  • Cousin of Mr. Bennet
  • Will receive Mr. Bennet's property when he dies
  • Clergyman at Lady de Bourgh's estate
  • Thinks he's very important (kind of cocky)
  • Mr. Darcy
  • Wealthy
  • From Derbyshire
  • Starts off being very rude, but changes for Elizabeth, who he falls in love with
  • Helps out Elizabeth's sister, when she denies the marriage proposal from Wickham, and with this help, Elizabeth eventual falls in love with him
  • Charles Bingley
  • First suitor introduced in the book
  • Moves three miles away from the Bennet's
  • Falls in love with Jane
  • He gets talked out of his love for Jane by her sisters and Mr. Darcy
  • Miss Bingley
  • Likes Mr. Darcy
  • Very good looking
  • Shallow
  • Fake with Jane and Elizabeth, pretends to be friends with them but talks crap about them behind their backs
  • Tries to keep Charles Bingley away from Jane
