Book Summary

  • So basically...
  • Pride and Prejudice is a novel about the romance between Elizabeth Bennet and Mr. Darcy and the trials and obstacles their relationship faces due to each other's pride and prejudice. Elizabeth is born into a fictional middle class family and has a traditional upbringing for her time period. The novel follows Elizabeth's experiences with a man, Mr. Darcy, who is from a higher social class than her. Elizabeth and Darcy both have initial preconceptions about each other, as Elizabeth views Darcy as rather snobbish, and Darcy doesn't see Elizabeth as for who she really is beyond her social class. The two develop a mutual love for one another, yet it takes the whole novel for the relationship to evolve, as they must struggle against many obstacles that stand in the way of their love.
